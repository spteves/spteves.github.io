Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. They are versatile, nutritious, and can be prepared in many different ways. One of the simplest and most delicious ways to enjoy eggs is by making scrambled eggs. This recipe will guide you through the process of making perfect scrambled eggs every time.

Ingredients

Method
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream
  1. Melt butter in fryinf pan over a medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs.
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds(as if you are making an omelette).
  5. When the eggs are still mist remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate until served).
